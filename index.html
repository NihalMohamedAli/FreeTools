BoldSign processes PDFs entirely in your browser. Most competitors (iLovePDF, Smallpdf, Adobe) upload your files to their servers. With BoldSign, your data stays on your device — better privacy, faster processing, no upload wait time.

With BoldSign, yes. Your documents never leave your device, making it safe for confidential contracts, medical records, financial statements, and legal documents. Learn more about browser-based safety.

Yes. Reorder files or arrange individual pages as needed. Preview the sequence before finalizing to make sure everything looks right.

No. The merged file retains the original quality, formatting, fonts, and layout of your source documents.
